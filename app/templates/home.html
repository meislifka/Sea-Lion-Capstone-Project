Tracking Endangered Galapagos Sea Lions

MACHINE LEARNING & CITIZEN SCIENCE & CONSERVATION RESEARCH

SeeLion uses an altered deepstack facial recognition algorithm to uniquely identify the faces of Galapagos sea lions. By assigning unique identifiers to each sea lion, researches can analyze the migration patterns and general tendencies of the species in the hopes of better understanding why they are endangered.

How to Use

On this site you will find three additional tabs: About, Register, and Submit Encounter. To learn more about Galapagos Sea Lions, the project background, and the intended use of the site, visit the 'About' tab. To submit an encounter, credentials are required. This prevents any unintended harm to the species, and ensures that SeeLion is as accurate and useful as possible. To obtain credentials, please visit the 'Register' tab and complete the form to request access to the encounter page.
